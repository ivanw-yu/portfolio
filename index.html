About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with expertise in developing websites using JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, React, Redux and Angular. In my experiences in web development, I have independently developed 4 websites including a question-and-answers posting application, a flashcard application, a photo sharing application and an organization website for the Chinese American Institute of Engineers and Scientists (CAIES).

I have also developed websites and software in teams for academic projects during my time in San Francisco State University, where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and achieved the Summa Cum Laude honors with a 3.87 GPA. I currently work for a community organization, West Bay Pilipino Multi-Service Center, where I mentor elementary, middle and high school youth, plan and facilitate academic enrichment and life skills workshops, and tutor students in subjects including Math, English and Computer Science (GitHub and Java).

I find joy in building things and it excites me to see websites grow from initial mock ups to finalized products. The consistent excitement I have for software development has motivated me to continually educate myself and take on big challenges in order to polish my skills and effective habits as a web developer.

My skills and experiences has allowed me to grow comfortable in developing websites by myself and in team environments. I'm currently looking for an entry-level software engineer role for a company where I can grow professionally and use my skills to contribute to team success.
